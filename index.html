System Overview

Senior Robotics Engineer at Earthsense with a focus on building reliable, production-grade autonomous systems that operate in real-world, unstructured environments.

My work spans the full autonomous navigation stack — from low-level sensor fusion and state estimation to high-level planning and control. I develop systems that must be robust to real-world constraints: sensor noise, GPS degradation, dynamic obstacles, and unpredictable terrain.

I specialize in bridging the gap between academic algorithms and production robotics — taking techniques like EKF, ES-EKF, and MPC from theory to deployed systems running in agricultural fields and complex outdoor environments.
